Project Overview

This project involved designing promotional backdrops for AI Training programs organized at Shri Kashi Vishwanath Temple. The visual style was developed to blend modern technology themes with the temple's cultural and spiritual identity.

Objective

Create impactful event branding that communicates the AI Training initiative while preserving the heritage and devotional significance of the temple environment.
